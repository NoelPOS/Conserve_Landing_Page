Why Conserve?

Conserve is the app designed to help you monitor and reduce your carbon footprint with ease. Through daily surveys, you can track your impact on the environment across key areas like transportation, food, and utilities. Stay motivated with badges and rewards as you make a positive change!

More info

Features

Personalized tracking: Tailored surveys measure your carbon footprint in transportation, food, and utilities.

Progress reports: Insights into your daily, weekly, and monthly carbon usage.

Reward system: Earn badges for meeting carbon limits and regular app engagement.

Daily Surveys
